
ZyFixer:
A plug-in to speedup the Zynamics IDA BinDiff plug-in by ~333% by filtering
out kernel32.dll->FlushFileBuffers() calls.

Author: Sirmabus 2013
http://www.sirmabus.macromonkey.com/
